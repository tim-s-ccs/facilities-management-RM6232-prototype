The steps shown below outline the process that you'll follow creating your facilites management procurement.

  1. Step 1 Basic Requirements

    We will first ask you for some basic service and contract information:

    • which services you want to have provided
    • where your buildings are located
    • your current annual cost

  2. Step 2 Results

    You will be shown:

    • a compliant sub-lot, and a shortlist of potential suppliers
    • the options available for your next steps if you want to proceed into a procurement

  3. Step 3 Further service and contract requirements (optional)

    You will be given the option of completing further building and service requirements either:

    • within this portal with us guiding you through the questions
    • using a spreadsheet template, which you can download, fill out the information, and upload

    Using this information we will be able to generate documents for you that you can use for your procurement

  4. Step 4 Final results

    On this page you will be able to download a copy of your search results.

    If you have added further service and contract requirements, you will be able to download:

    • a deliverables matrix
    • other spreadsheets
